Why should you do some baking this Christmas?

Christmas baking is the perfect blend of tradition and joy, filling kitchens with the warm, comforting scents of cinnamon, nutmeg, and fresh-baked cookies. It’s a chance to bond with loved ones, share laughter over sticky dough, and create treats that bring smiles to everyone. Plus, who can resist the magic of turning simple ingredients into festive masterpieces—especially when sneaking a taste of the batter is half the fun?
